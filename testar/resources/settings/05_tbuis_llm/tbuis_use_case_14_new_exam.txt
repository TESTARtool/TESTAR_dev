Teacher announces a new exam date for his/her subject

Credentials keen/pass
Condition New term can only be announced for a subject the teacher teaches
Condition Single subject can have maximum of 3 exam dates announced by a single teacher
Condition Number of possible participants cannot be lower than 1 and higher than 10
Condition The exam date cannot be announced for a date that has already passed
Condition The time between the last exam date and newly announced exam date has to be at least 24 hours
Condition The exam date must be written down in yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm format
Then New exam date has been successfully created!
